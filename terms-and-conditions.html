Terms and Conditions

Pre-order System

  • Our products are manufactured in batches when we reach a minimum order quantity (MOQ) of 20 units, with a target of 50 units per batch
  • The manufacturing cycle begins once MOQ is reached
  • Delivery occurs approximately 30 days after batch manufacturing begins

Payment Options

  • Full Payment: $35.99 paid upfront
  • Split Payment:
    • $20 initial deposit
    • $20 final payment due when product is ready for delivery

Batch Fulfillment

  • Pre-orders are queued until we reach the minimum batch size
  • You will be notified when:
  • Your pre-order is confirmed
  • The batch MOQ is reached
  • Manufacturing begins
  • Your product is ready for delivery

Refund Policy

  • Full refunds are provided if:
    • The minimum batch size is not reached within the specified timeframe
    • We are unable to fulfill manufacturing responsibilities
  • Note: This is not a return policy. All sales are final once the product is delivered

Local Pickup

  • Shipping is not included
  • Products must be picked up locally
  • You will be notified of pickup details when your product is ready

Order Communication

  • You'll receive an email confirmation with your order number after placing your pre-order
  • We'll send email updates when:
  • The batch minimum is reached
  • Manufacturing begins
  • Your product is ready for pickup
  • Please save your order number for all communications
