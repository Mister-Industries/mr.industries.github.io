Terms and Conditions

Pre-order System

  • Products without existing inventory are manufactured in batches when we reach a minimum order quantity (MOQ) of 20 units, with a target of 50 units per batch
  • The manufacturing cycle begins once MOQ is reached
  • Delivery occurs approximately 30 days after batch manufacturing begins

Payment Options

  • Full Starter Kit Payment: $75 + tax
  • Basic Kit Payment: $62.50 + tax

Batch Fulfillment

  • Pre-orders are queued until we reach the minimum batch size
  • You will be notified via email when:
  • Your pre-order is confirmed
  • The batch MOQ is reached
  • Manufacturing begins
  • Your product is ready for delivery

Refund Policy

  • Full refunds are provided if the minimum batch size is not reached within the specified timeframe
  • Note: We do NOT have a return policy. All sales are final once the product is delivered.

Shipping

  • Products are shipped within 14 days, or once batch manufacturing completes
  • Products will be shipped via USPS Ground Advantage or UPS Ground
  • You will be notified of tracking details once your product has shipped

Local Pickup

  • Products must be picked up locally (near CU Boulder campus)
  • You will be notified of pickup details when your product is ready
  • We will contact you to schedule a pickup time and location for your product

Order Communication

  • You'll receive an email confirmation with your order number after placing your pre-order
  • We'll send email updates when:
  • The batch minimum is reached
  • Manufacturing begins
  • Your product has shipped or is ready for pickup
  • Please save your order number for all communications
